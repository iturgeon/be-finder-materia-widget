Instructions

You can use this Be Finder to locate "be" verbs in your text--useful for writing in e-prime and for revising any of your writing. This tool won't notice "be" verbs that are contracted, however.

For example, it won't flag "it's" even though "it's" = "it is." You'll need to find contractions on your own. Try using your word processor's "find" function to search for 's , 'm , and 're.
